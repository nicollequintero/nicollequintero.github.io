Values

August 10, 2014

When I've been my happiest or proudest in life, the words that come to mind are:

  • Affection
  • Compassion
  • Helping other people
  • Honesty

While there are a lot of values that I admire and aspire to, what everything really comes down to for me is people. Therefore, all of the values I associated with the best in life are social and helping centric. Things, ambition, external rewards or accolades are nice, but I suspect not what I'll be the most proud of when my days are numbered. Instead, I'd like to reflect and know that I helped other people be their best selves and did the same.

If I'm feeling down or threatned, what would probably help the most is finding these values in other people. Feeling affection and compassion from someone. Recieving help or advice from someone who can be honest and kind about my situation.

The last time I gave advice it was related to how to navigate a tricky friendship and control issue. The way I approached it was to advise my friend to be honest about her feelings, try to see things from the other side as opposed to viewing all actions as a personal affront, and to propose a solution that required compromise from both parties.
